Atlas SRE

Local First Incident Intelligence

Operational Knowledge,
instantly accessible.

Atlas SRE is a specialized NotebookLM-style platform for Site Reliability Engineers. It unifies your postmortems, RCAs, and architecture diagrams into a single, queryable local intelligence workspace.

See Architecture

Strictly Local RAG Pipeline

Atlas operates entirely within your browser to ensure security and zero latency for your most sensitive operational documentation.

1. Ingest

Drop your PDFs, RCAs, and diagrams directly into the secure workspace.

2. Index

Local TF-IDF lexical engine chunks and indexes text entirely client-side.

3. Retrieve

Lightning-fast keyword overlap scoring fetches the most relevant context.

4. Generate

Gemini synthesizes a cited response using only your provided context.

Core Capabilities

AI Incident Investigator

A specialized chat interface designed for querying complex operational history. Ask about recent incidents, root causes, or general system architecture, and receive direct answers.

  • Explicit Source Citations
  • Embedded Document Viewer
  • Configurable LLM Parameters

Local Knowledge Base

Your private repository of documentation. Upload PDFs seamlessly, and let the local engine handle text extraction, chunking, and indexing without exposing sensitive data.

  • Preloaded SRE Demo Dataset
  • Drag & Drop Ingestion
  • Real-time Lexical Search
